About Us

EcoClean is dedicated to spreading awareness about pollution and encouraging community cleaning initiatives.

We organize events to clean rivers, beaches, parks, and public spaces.

Our mission is to create a cleaner, healthier environment for everyone.

Events

"Clean surroundings, healthy living." Our upcoming cleaning awareness events are designed to bring our community together for a cleaner and greener tomorrow.

"Be the change you wish to see — start by cleaning your street." Join us as volunteers gather to clean public areas, parks, and roads.

"A clean city reflects the mindset of its people." Through our events, we aim to educate everyone about waste management, recycling, and sustainable habits.
